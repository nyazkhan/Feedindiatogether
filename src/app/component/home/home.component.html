How to help ?

You can list yourself as a volunteer. And choose how you want to help be it feeding people with a home-cooked meal or help them in taking shelter or any other way.

Once you are listed you will be contacted by authorities or front line workers who are authorised to work outside.

They will ask for your help as you have listed if there’s any requirement.

Please note that becoming a volunteer does not mean that you should leave your home. You should stay at home. Any help you can provide should be while you are staying at home.
